Meet Agent

Agent is an AI assistant that takes real actions for you. It can write code, send email, organize files, schedule things, and browse the web. You delegate a task; Agent does it.

About this study

In this study, you'll use Agent to complete a series of short tasks. The tasks that follow are scenarios our research team has prepared to see how the agent handles a range of common requests. Some may resemble everyday personal tasks; others relate to the study itself. For each task, make the decisions you would with your own accounts and data.

How it works
  • You'll complete 10 short tasks with Agent (about 10 to 15 minutes).
  • To keep the experience consistent across participants, the tasks are pre-written. You'll see each one in a pop-up and click "Send" to give it to Agent. There's no chat input.
  • At some points, Agent may ask you to confirm a step before continuing. Respond as you naturally would.
  • A quick overview of the chat controls will appear when you get started.
